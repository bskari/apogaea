Piddle
======

Sound reactive LED strips, originating from a central point and placed in a
geodesic dome. Running on a custom PCB with ESP32. Coming soon: ArtNet DMX!

Wiring
------

- LED pins: see source
- Microphone
  - SCK: 19
  - WS: 22
  - SD: 21
  - L/R: ground
  - VDD: 3.3V
  - GND: ground

Hardware
--------

- ESP32 WROOM 32
- I2S microphone
- 10 WS2812B LED strips
